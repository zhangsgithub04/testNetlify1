Version 2 Grammar of a tinny language (expression, term and factor, unambigious exponentiation )

  1. EXPR -> TERM {(+|-) TERM}
  2. TERM -> FACTOR {(*|/) FACTOR}
  3. FACTOR -> BASE {[ ^ exponent ]}
  4. base -> '(' expr ')' | identifier | number
  5. exponent -> '(' expr ')' | identifier | number
Pay attention that this grammar likely is wrong with respect to the power operation, because no consideration of the right association of the power operator:(. This is for illustration purpose of expanded grammar. Next version will support right association!
